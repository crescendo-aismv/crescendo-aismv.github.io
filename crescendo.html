About the event

Crescendo is an Inter-School Western Music Competition organised by the WMS for 14 years now. Action Packed two days, with 200 young musicians from 25 schools from all over Delhi participating in the categories- Band, Choir, Duet and Solo Singing, Crescendo is a much awaited event for everyone.

More about Crescendo

Adjudged by the best musicians, it is a healthy competition in which talented musicians battle it out to win the coveted All Rounder trophy. Participants of classes 6-12 from all over Delhi NCR come together to showcase their ability to enrapture the audience and dazzle the judges.

Why you should join us

Taking people's breath away each year, we are all set for another season of talent hunting, meeting new people, making new friends and having fun with music.
