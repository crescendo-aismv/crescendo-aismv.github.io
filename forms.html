SOLO SINGING AND INSTRUMENTAL SOLO COMPETITION (9-12)

Virtuoso for classes 9-12 is here with its virtual edition. Allow this platform to serve as the catalyst that helps you to arouse an interest to explore the world of music and tunes. Rules and Regulation for AUDITION Round:-

1. Solo Singing


• Record a video of yourself singing ANY ENGLISH SONG of your choice with live vocals.
• Video should be recorded ONLY in SCHOOL UNIFORM.
• (New admissions can wear casuals, if uniform is unavailable)
• Ensure that your voice is clearly audible and your whole face is visible throughout the video.
• Karoke tracks can be used as backing tracks to assist the vocals. Or, students can also play the instruments themselves while singing. Furthermore, students can also get an acoustic track (piano/guitar) recorded if required.
• The video should not exceed 2 minutes. If done so, the entry will not be considered. (Choose the best 2 minutes of the track )
The above time limit is only applicable to the audition round

2. Instrumental Solo

• Record a video of yourself playing ANY piece of your choice (Western Pieces ONLY) on your respective instrument.
• Video should be recorded ONLY in SCHOOL UNIFORM.
• (New admissions can wear casuals, if uniform is unavailable)
• Ensure that the instrument is clearly audible and, the whole instrument as well as the playing technique is visible throughout the video.
• No Backing Track should be audible in the video. However, backing tracks can be played in headphones to assist while playing.
• Metronome can be used by the students.
• The video should not exceed 2 minutes. If done so, the entry will not be considered.
• LIST OF INSTRUMENTS THAT CAN BE PLAYED-
a. Electric/ Acoustic Guitar
b. Keyboard/Piano
c. Drums
d. Bass
e. Any Unique Instrument (Flute, Violin, Cajon, Congo, Xylophone, Trumpet etc. )

SUBMISSION OF ENTRIES-


• Once your video is recorded, upload it on Drive and turn on link sharing.
• Once done, fill the form below and submit the link to your entry.
you can only submit the form once your video has been recorded and uploaded on drive and not before that.
In case, you face any problem in uploading and generating link of the Drive file, refer to this video Or any other commonly found tutorials on YouTube.

Note-

1. The audition entry shall be judged. After which, shortlisted students for each category will be contacted for the live competition to be held on 29th June 2021.
2. Last date of submission for AUDITION ENTRY is 18th June 2021.
3. The same song/piece performed in audition round will be performed during the live competition, if selected.
4. In case of a tie in judgement in Audition Round, a second round may be held for those particular students.
5. In case, the student is selected, he/she shall be added to a WhatsApp group by 25th June. In case the student is not added to the group by the above date, then it indicates that he/she did not make it to the next round.
