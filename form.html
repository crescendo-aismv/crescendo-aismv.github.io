Application Form

With the aim to select and nurture the best, we, Crescendo open admissions to our society for the session 2022-23!
If you feel you have an eye for creativity and are eager to get a spot in, do fill the form below.
But before that, kindly go through these important instructions-

ADMISSION CRITERIA
For classes 6th-10th
-Open for all

For classes 11 & 12
-New admissions who have joined this year or in the academic session 2021-22

IMPORTANT INSTRUCTIONS-
- After submission of the form, it will be reviewed by us and shortlisted students will be contacted for the second round.

NOTE-Parents of the selected students will need to sign a Declaration stating that their ward is signing up for the society under parental consent and that he/she shall abide by the society's rules.
