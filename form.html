Application Form

During this global pandemic, we all are under quarantine. However, we wouldn't let it stop us from giving a platform to eager musicians and very talented students to showcase their abilites.
Hence, we-Crescendo, are opening admissions to our society!
If you are eager to get a spot in, do fill the admission form below.
But before that, kindly go through the important instructions given below-

ADMISSION CRITERIA
For classes 6-10
-Only those students who opted for Western Music MTD in the previous session
-New admissions who have joined the school this year

For classes 11 & 12
-New admissions who have joined this year

IMPORTANT INSTRUCTIONS-
- After submission of the form, it will be reviewed by us and shortlisted students will be contacted for the second round.

NOTE-Parents of the selected students will need to sign a Declaration stating that their ward is signing up for the society under parental consent and that he/she shall abide by the society's rules.
