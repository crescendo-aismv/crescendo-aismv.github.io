Rockshow

Our fundraiser for the youth power of the school
This is the perfect platform for the students to display and showcase their talents

What its all about

A highly anticipated event of the year amongst the students, Rockshow is all about dance, music and fun.

Why the school enjoys it so much

Organised annually, it is an event full of energy, liveliness and verve. Rockshow has an interesting line up of school's band as well as professional bands captivating the audience with evergreen and catchy songs which gets everyone on their feet.
