Meet the Team

Presenting the Crescendo Team for 2023-2024

Arnav Gupta - Secretary

Arnav Gupta has an incredible eye for creativity. He is the leader of Crescendo's IT Team and is the mind behind it's beautiful presentations and artworks. He also renders his voice for the school choir, which makes him the perfect candidate for this position.

Ananya Sharma - Treasurer

Ananya Sharma is one of the Duet Singers of AISMV who leads the harmonies sections. The team looks up to her impeccable organizational skills and outstanding musicianship. She has won many laurels for AISMV and continues to be an integral part of the society.
