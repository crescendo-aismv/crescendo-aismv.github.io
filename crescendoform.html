Rules and Guidelines

CRESCENDO 2021


The Western Music Society of Amity International School, Mayur Vihar proudly calls upon talented individuals, to foregather at the 15th edition of the most awaited musical extravaganza- Crescendo, a competition to give a stage to your endless potential.

GENERAL RULES AND REGULATIONS:


1. Participants should be in their regular school uniform or it will lead to negative marking. (New admissions are exempted)
2. Registration is open from 3 rd July 2021.
3. The registration must be done by 15th July 2021 (Thursday).
4. Students from classes 7th -12th (as of academic session 2021-22) can compete in the competition.
5. A picture (front and back) of the school ID card is to be submitted while registering on the website. (ID Card of academic session 2019-20 or Report Card of academic session 2020-21 are accepted)
6. For updates, follow Crescendo’s official Instagram or Facebook page.
7. Winners will be given e-certificate through mail.
8. Host School will compete o n l y in the Instrumental Solo Category.
9. Kindly note that only one registration form must be filled by each school.
10. All participants must strictly adhere to the above mentioned rules. The decisionof the judges will be final and binding. All schools are requested to respect the same.

LIVE EVENTS


INSTRUMENTAL SOLO CATEGORY

Judgment Criteria:


(i)Tonal Quality (ii)Overall Performance (iii) Technicality.

Rules And Regulations for instrumentalists


1. No sequence, prerecorded music or backing tracks should be audible to the judges, if so the participant shall be disqualified from that category. However, backing track can be played in headphones to assist the participant while performing.
2. Metronome can be used by the participants.
3. Time allotted to every student is 2 minutes.
4. Please go through the rules and time limit very carefully. Adhering to time is compulsory.
On exceeding 10 seconds, 10 marks will be deducted and on exceeding 15 seconds, the participant will be disqualified.
5. Only one participant per school is allowed for each instrument. (E.g. Only Drummer A from XYZ School can participate in the drumming category and another drummer from XYZ School cannot participate in the same category.)
6. For keyboard and guitar, digital sounds can be used.
Kindly ensure that the camera angle is such that the judges are able to see the playing
7. technique as well as the participant with ease.
8. The following sub categories will be there-

i) Electric Drums
ii) Acoustic Drums
iii) Keyboard
iv) Piano
v) Bass
vi) Electric Guitar
vii) Acoustic Guitar
viii) Unique Instruments
8. After registration, participants of each category will be contacted by the
organizing team.
9. A trial run for each category shall be scheduled a few days prior to the event for which details will be given post registration.
10. This trial run shall acquaint the participants with MS Teams so that no problems are faced on the day of the event.

PRE-RECORDED EVENTS


ORIGINAL COMPOSITION

This pandemic, along with its adversities, has forced us to stay away from a thing which we used to cherish every day in school- Friends. With Friendship day around the corner, we invite Original Compositions dedicated to friends, friendships, and the year we all lost due to the pandemic. So, get into your creative groove, pen down impactful lyrics and design some harmonious melodies.


Judgment Criteria:


(i)Originality (ii) Composition (iii) Lyrics

Rules And Regulations for Original Composition


1. Students are required to record an audio track of their original song on the given theme.
2. Lyrics of the song are also required to be submitted in a PDF file.
3. Submission of the track & lyrics-
(i) Upload the track on Drive and provide the access to the same.
(ii) Email containing Submission Entry Link and Lyrics PDF should be sent on Crescendo’s Official Email ID (competition@crescendowms.com) latest by 25th July 2021.
(iii) The subject of the Email should be “Entry for Original Composition”.
(iv) Mention the following in the body of the email-
(1) Full school name
(2) Names and Classes of participants involved in the making of the OC
(3) Title of the Song
4. The track should not exceed 4 minutes. On exceeding the time limit, the entry will not be judged.
(The composition need not be very technical; a simple acoustic song with lyrics is also very well acceptable.)

SOLO SINGING

Judgment Criteria:


(i)Voice Quality (ii) Vocal Modulation & Improvisation (iii) Overall Performance (iv) Synchronization (v) Song Selection

Rules And Regulations for Solo Singing


1) The participants are required to shoot a video with live vocals. If any discrepancy is found in the synchronization of the audio and video, it will lead to disqualification.
2) The video and audio track should not be digitally recorded.
3) NO karaoke track will be allowed however participants can download a piano/guitar track or the same can also be recorded by accompanists of their respective school. The singer can also play while singing.
4) The video should not exceed 5 minutes. On exceeding the time limit, the entry will not be judged.
5) Submission of the video-
(i) Upload the video on Drive and provide the access to the same.
(ii) Email containing Submission Entry Link should be sent on Crescendo’s Official Email ID (competition@crescendowms.com) latest by 25th July 2021.
(iii) The subject of the Email should be “Entry for Solo Singing”
(iv) Mention the following in the body of the email-
(1)Full school name
(2)Names and Classes of participant
(3)Title of the Song Sung
6) Only one entry per school is allowed.
