Meet the Team

Presenting the Crescendo Team for 2021-2022

Arindam Nath - President

An IT genius , Arindam Nath has been a vital part of the society for the past 4 years. He is also a gifted guitarist. From designing the posters to editing the intricate videos for the events, Arindam Nath has played a key role in the functioning of the society and is the perfect fit for this post.

Debaditya Halder - Vice President

He is an extraordinary pianist and vocalist. He has been a part of the society and the band for 4 years and has won numerous awards for Crescendo. He has claimed 2 best pianists to his name. Apart from being an exceptional musician, his amicable personality and diligence towards the society makes him a great vice president.

Meenalika Singh - Secretary

Meenalika's dedication and passion towards music makes her a perfect fit for the post of Secretary. Her powerful vocals and melodious voice have always proved to be an asset to the choir. With a flair for writing, Meenalika has certainly contributed to the society in more ways than one, making her an essential team member and a noteworthy leader.
