Meet the Team

In 2016, the founders of Crescendo first lit the spark to the fire which is flourishing even today. The legacy of Crescendo started with just 10 members which has quadrapled to accomadate more than 40 members in the current team. We owe a debt to our alumni for founding this society which has opened a treasure of opportunities for many eager future members. Following is the team from the inital batch of crescendo

Sankalp Biswal- President, 2016-17

Sankalp Biswal is a proficient guitarist who has secured the individual prize for Best Guitarist 13 times which still remains unbeaten in the delhi circuit. He lead the society as President and won numerous laurels for the school in various competitions during his tenure here.

Mana Sharma- Vice President, 2016-17

Mana Sharma, possesses excellent organisational skills and outstanding leadership qualities. Her camaraderie with fellow team members allowed her to successful organise numerous events as the Vice President of the society.

Sanchi Gupta- Secretary, 2016-17

Sanchi Gupta is an extremely talented artist who is able to paint exquisite works of art with her brushes. Along with heading the Art Departement of the society, she lead the team as the Secretary with her exceptional management skills.
