Our Pillars of Strength

Our guiding spirits, backing us at every step

Ashley Vaz

Mr Ashley Vaz, a phenomenal musician himself and great teacher whom every student looks up to. He believes in reaching milestones with practice and hard work. Always encouraging his students to strive for excellence and making music their passion. From starting Crescendo to shaping it to what it is today, sir has been our pillar of support throughout this journey.

Sarolea Mesquita (Alesha)

Our WM teacher, Ms Sarolea Mesquita has taught us in more ways than one. Specialising in vocals, her tips and techniques have allowed many of our budding vocalists to excel. Her ability to view each student's potential uniquely has helped our team recognise fresh talent in our school. A mentor, a motivator and a friend are the perfect words to describe her. We are forever grateful to be under maam's tutelage.

Sunita Chopra

Ms Sunita Chopra, Event coordinator of our school, has carried us through all our events. Her impeccable administration has ensured well organised and uninterrupted events. She's always on her toes making events hassle-free and experience loaded with fun. She's always approachable and ready for something new, her efficiency never fails to amaze us.
