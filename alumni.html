Sankalp Biswal

Sankalp Biswal, is a proficient guitarist who has secured the individual prize for Best Guitarist 13 times which still remains unbeaten in the Delhi Circuit. Being the lead guitarist of the school band he brought home countless awards during his time at AISMV. Currently he is pursuing his master's in AI from Northeastern University, Boston.

Nishant Iyer

Nishant Iyer, has been playing bass professionally for over a decade now. He also cleared ECA trials due to his immense talent in the field of music. He has rendered his skills to bands like Simple Truth, Havens Down, Shockblast, Trouble Clef and Jeremiah 29:11 to name a few.

Maulshree Kumar

Maulshree was the soloist of AISMV and has claimed many individual prizes for Best Vocalist to her name. One of the best solo singers the Delhi Music Circuit has ever witnessed, she also cleared ECA trials and got admission in Lady Shri Ram, Delhi University. She went on to become the youngest secretary and further the President of the western music society in her college.

Samarth Iyer

Samarth Iyer, an alumni of the school, from the batch of 2018, is an astounding vocalist. He was the vocalist of AISMV for solo, duet and band categories and was the tenor in the school choir and won many laurels for the school. Currently, in addition to pursuing his music interest as a playback singer, he is pursuing sound engineering from the Asian Academy of Film and Television.

Armaan Afzal

Armaan, who headed the society as President for two consecutive years, has won several laurels at inter and intra school events for AISMV and has also been a 2 time Yamaha Teens Rock Contest finalist which is a National level music competition. Currently, he is pursuing his higher studies at Stockholm School of Economics, Sweden where he received a scholarship based on his remarkable music portfolio.

Devashish Gupta

Devashish Gupta, from the batch of 2020, was the lead guitarist of the school band. In addition to claiming the award for Best Guitarist 3 times and several others, he represented Delhi at the nationals of Yamaha teens rock contest held in Bangalore. He received admission in the University of Manchester due to his accolades as a guitarist in school.

Shagun Malhotra

Shagun Malhotra, along with being the Vice President of the society, was the Harmonist of the choir and brought laurels by winning 2nd position at DPS, RK Puram. She received admission in Ashoka University due to her achievements in the field of Western Music.

Pranay Mathur

Pranay, an alumni from the year 2020, rendered his skills as the flutist and saxophone player of the band. He is currently completing his masters from Imperial College London. He has received several individual awards over the past years, such as 1st place in Symphony NSIT and MAMC light Indian instrumental competitions as well as with his band in competitions held in Christ University, AIIMS Delhi, Jaipuria College and many more.

Debaditya Halder

He is an extraordinary pianist and vocalist. He was a part of the society and the band for 5 years and won numerous awards for Crescendo. He is an ABRSM grade 6 pianist and has claimed 2 best pianists to his name. He is currently studying at Hindu College, DU.
