Presenting Crescendo Alumnis

Armaan Afzal

Armaan, who headed the society as President for two consecutive years, has won several laurels at inter and intra school events for AISMV and has also been a 2 time Yamaha Teens Rock Contest finalist which is a National level music competition. Currently, he is pursuing his higher studies at Stockholm School of Economics, Sweden where he received a scholarship based on his remarkable music portfolio.

Pranay Mathur

Pranay, an alumni from the year 2020, rendered his skills as the flutist and saxophone player of the band. He is currently completing his masters from Imperial College London. He has received several individual awards over the past years, such as 1st place in Symphony NSIT and MAMC light Indian instrumental competitions as well as with his band in competitions held in Christ University, AIIMS Delhi, Jaipuria College and many more.

Aakriti Bharadwaj

Aakriti Bharadwaj was from the batch of 2016, who bagged several laurels for AISMV during her tenure, in solo singing and choir competitions. She received an admission to Delhi University dure to her proficiency in music and is currently working as a Content Curator for the entire international catalog with Wynk Music, which includes major record labels like Universal Music, Sony Music and much more

Devashish

Devashish Gupta, from the batch of 2020, was the lead guitarist of the school band. He received admission in the University of Manchester due to his accolades as a guitarist in school.
