Meet the Team

Presenting the Crescendo Team for 2023-2024

Tarini Arora - President

Tarini Arora is an articulate singer who also renders her skills for the school band- Odd one out. She is one with an amicable personality who creates a welcoming atmosphere for the junior. Her management skills are praiseworthy and an asset to the society.

Sidhi Sharma - Vice President

Sidhi Sharma is blessed with the art of singing and is the soloist of AISMV & the lead vocalist of the girl band- Odd One Out. Her speaking and leadership skills make her a worthy recipient of this post. She aspires to take the team to new heights under her guidance.

Ridhi Gupta - Secretary

Ridhi Gupta has an incredible eye for creativity. he is the leader of Crescendo's IT Team and is the mind behind it's beautiful presentations and artworks. he also renders her voice for the school choir, which makes her the perfect candidate for this position.

Pushkar - Joint Secretary

Pushkar is one of the Duet Singers of AISMV who leads the harmonies sections. The team looks up to her impeccable organizational skills and outstanding musicianship. She has won many laurels for AISMV and continues to be an integral part of the society.
