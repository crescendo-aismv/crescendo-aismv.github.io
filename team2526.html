Meet the Team

Presenting the Crescendo Team for 2024-2025

Pushkar - President

Apart from being a dedicated and talented student, Pushkar excels in IT and technology, making him an asset to the society beyond just music. Over the past three years, he has played a key role in expanding and enhancing Crescendo’s website, Instagram page, and overall design, ensuring a strong digital presence. His contributions to event organization and logistics have been invaluable, demonstrating his leadership and problem-solving skills. With his deep understanding of both the creative and technical aspects of the society, he is well-suited to lead Crescendo to new heights as its President.

Prisha Naithani - Vice President

Prisha is a sharp and well-organized member of the society, with a keen ability to manage logistics and ensure smooth execution of events. She has a strong analytical mindset, making her excellent at problem-solving and adapting to new challenges. Her ability to coordinate with team members efficiently, coupled with her collaborative spirit, makes her an integral part of Crescendo. Prisha values the relationships she builds within the team, always fostering a positive and productive environment.

Divya Chandna - Secretary

Divya is a meticulous and detail-oriented individual who approaches every task with perfection and dedication. Her ability to stay organized under pressure and manage multiple responsibilities has made her an essential pillar of the society. She has played a crucial role in supporting teams, assisting members, and overseeing event planning, ensuring that every aspect runs seamlessly. Her strategic thinking and problem-solving skills make her an excellent fit for the role of Secretary. With her strong work ethic and commitment, she aims to continue to enhance the success of Crescendo.

Shivika Gupta - Joint Secretary

Having joined Crescendo three years ago, Shivika has consistently demonstrated passion, creativity, and leadership in everything she does. For the past two years, she has led the society’s art team, ensuring that every event is not just well-organized but also visually stunning. Beyond her artistic contributions, she's a confident person believing in values like teamwork and punctuality along with guiding and uplifting those around her. As Joint Secretary, she aims to bring fresh ideas and creative energy to Crescendo while ensuring the society continues to thrive.

Daksh Mittal - Treasurer

Daksh is a dedicated and versatile member of Crescendo, always eager to take on new challenges and improve his skills. His leadership qualities help him connect with his team, fostering collaboration, mutual growth and learning. Beyond his organizational abilities, Daksh is skilled in IT and design, contributing to posters and visuals for various events. A confident orator and effective team director, he is well-suited for the role of Treasurer, ensuring Crescendo’s management and visual presentation remain top-notch.
