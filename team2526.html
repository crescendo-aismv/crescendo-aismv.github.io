Meet the Team

Presenting the Crescendo Team for 2024-2025

Tarini Arora - President

In addition to being the bassist of the school band, Tarini’s organizational skills and exceptional leadership quality is what makes her a perfect fit to head the team as the President. She has won several laurels making the school proud on countless occasions and continues to work as an inherent part of the society.

Sidhi Sharma - Vice President

Sidhi has been a part of the society for the past 6 years and is a potent member of the team possessing a brilliant work ethic. From making beautiful presentations to live-streaming our events, she has been an instrumental part of the team and one that everyone looks up to for her dedication.

Ridhi Sharma - Secretary

Ridhi’s charismatic and committed way of completing her duties is unparalleled. She is the leader of the PR team and is the genius behind Crescendo’s social handles. Her eye for detail and diligence makes her a perfect fit for the post of Secretary.

Pushkar - Joint Secretary

Pushkar has been a part of the society for the past 2 years and is blessed with exceptional IT skills. From creating stunning posters to editing the official website, he’s done it all making him an integral part of the society and a perfect fit for the post. The team looks up to his artistry and creativity.
