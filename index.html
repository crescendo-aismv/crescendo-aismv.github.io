About us

Crescendo is the Western Music Society of Amity International School Mayur Vihar. Being one of the most reputed societies in the Delhi NCR, Crescendo has been hosting successful events for over a decade now. Crescendo is a team of more than 50 students, each bringing their unique talent to the table and adding to its distinctiveness. The team ranges from vocalists and instrumentalists to designers and IT specialists. Each member’s effort and contribution adds up, making each event even better than the last.
